Butter Tofu

Tofu butter masala is a vegan meal that use cripsy tofu and delightful Indian curry gravy. Easily one of my favorite dishes to eat!

While this dish is vegan the tofu can be switched out for any meat protein.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 14 ounces Firm Tofu
  • 1 cup water
  • cilantro to granish

For gravy

  • 2 small tomatoes
  • 1 small onion
  • 4 small 1/2 in pieces cinnamon
  • 4 cloves
  • 2 cardamom
  • 1/4 cup cashews
  • 1 green chili or to taste

Spices

  • 2 tsp coriander seeds powder
  • 1 tsp tumeric powder
  • salt to taste
  • Red chili powder to taste

Instructions

  1. Drain tofu of water and cut into pieces.
  2. Pan fry pieces with oil until golden brown.
  3. Grind all gravy ingredients into a smooth paste.
  4. Turn on insta-pot in saute mode: high.
    5. Add butter, once hot add cumin seeds. Once cumin seeds are well fried, add ginger garlic paste and fry for 2 to 3 mins.
  5. Add gravy to insta-pot, mix, and cook for 5 to 7 mins.
  6. Add all other ingredients and tofu.
  7. Put on lid and pressure cook for 2 mins on high.
  8. Use natural release or quick release after 10 mins in warm mode.
  9. Serve with Naan or rice.
