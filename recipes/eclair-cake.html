Eclair Cake

Eclair Cake is a dessert that is incredibly easy to make but equally tasty. It is as easy as making pudding!!!

This is a great recipe for someone who is just starting out with baking but yet wants to still bring something to a gathering of friends or family. Note: Allow for at least 12 hours in the Refirgerator.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounce package graham crackers
  • 2 packages instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1 (16 ounce) package frozen whipped topping
  • 1 (16 ounce) package prepared chocolate frosting

Instructions

  1. Line bottom of 9x13 baking pan with graham crackers.
  2. In Large bowl, combine the milk and vanilla pudding according to directions on box.
  3. Fold in whipped topping to pudding mixture.
  4. Spread layer of pudding ontop of graham crackers.
  5. Alternate layers of graham crackers and vanilla pudding, making sure to end with layer of graham crackers.
  6. Cook the container of frosting uncovered in microwave for 1 min.
  7. Pour over top of cake.
  8. Refirgerate for at least 12 hours.
